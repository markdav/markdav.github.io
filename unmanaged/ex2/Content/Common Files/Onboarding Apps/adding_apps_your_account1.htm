Adding Apps to your account

From the app catalog, you can add any app listed to your account. You can also think of the Add functionality as app onboarding.  Use the following steps to add apps to your account.

  1. From the HPE GreenLake CCS console home page, click the ExploreCatalog link on the AppCatalog tile.

  2. On the App Catalog page, select the app catalog region from the drop-down list.

  3. Select AllRegions to show all the apps available in the catalog.

Figure 1. Regions Drop-down

 

  1. Click the Add button next to the app you want to add to your account.

Figure 2. HPE GreenLake CCS Catalog app

Note:

If you are authorized to add the selected app, a successful provisioning message displays. Suppose you do not have the authorization to add or load this app into your account, a failed provisioning message displays. Contact your CCS account administrator for help.

After an app is provisioned, the app is added to your MyApp page.
