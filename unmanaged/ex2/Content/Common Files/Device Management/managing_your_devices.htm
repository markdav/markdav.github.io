Managing Your Devices

HPE GreenLake Common Cloud Services (CSS) device management section allows you to view and manage, and onboard all the devices in your account from the device management page. If the device you purchased does not show up in your account, you can manually add it.

Note: Users having roles with Modify permission can add devices. Users having roles with View Only permission can only view the Device Account module.

Viewing Devices

The devices provisioned in your account are listed in the Account Home > QuickStart > Device Management page. A dashboard lists the total number of devices and the number assigned & licensed, the number of devices requiring assignments, and licenses in the account.

The following table describes the columns in the Devices table.

Parameter Description
Serial Number The serial number of the device.
Model Hardware model of the device.
MAC Address MAC address of the device.
Application Application assigned to the device.
License Tier License tier assigned to the device.
