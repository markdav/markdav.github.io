Onboarding Devices

There are two methods for adding device you can manage from your account.

  • Manually Onboarding Devices
  • Devices added by HPE Cloud Administrators

Manually Onboarding Devices

You can manually add a device to your account by selecting the device type and providing the device detail, such as the serial number and Entitlement ID. For more information, see Adding Devices. The process for onboarding devices includes the following activity.

  • Adding Devices
  • Assigning Devices
  • Applying License to Devices
  • Adding License Keys to Account

Adding Devices

Use the following steps to add devices to your HPE GreenLake Common Cloud account.

Using the Setup Wizard

  1. On the Account Home page, click Device Management. The Device Management (Inventory) displays.
  2. Click Add Devices. The Add Devices pop-up window displays.
  3. Select the Type and add the Serial Number and Entitlement ID of the devices.

    4. Note: You can find the serial number of devices on the front or back of the hardware.

  4. Click Done.
  5. Review the devices in your account.

Assigning Devices

The Assigned & License section enables you to assign, update, or even unassign a license from a device.

Use the following steps to assign devices to application instances.

  1. On the Account Home page, under Device Management, click Devices Require Assignment. The list of devices requiring assignments displays.
  2. Select the device(s) you want to assign to an application.
  3. Click Assign to Application. The Assign Device Application Instance screen displays.
  4. Select the Application and then select the Application Instance for each device.
  5. Click Next. A message indicating your Devices are Assigned displays. You can view the changes you made in the Activity section.
  6. Click Finish.

Unassigning Devices

  1. On the Account Home page, under Device Management, click Assigned & Licensed. The list of all the devices with assignments and licenses in your account displays.
  2. Select the device(s) you want to unassign.
  3. Click Remove Assignment.

    4. A confirming message displays asking if you are sure you wish to proceed with removing these application assignments. Removing assignments will also automatically remove the licenses for those same devices.

  4. Click Remove Assignment again to continue.

Applying Licenses to Devices

All devices must have a license key assign for you to activate and use it.

Use the following steps to apply license keys to devices.

  1. On the Account Home page, under Device Management, click Devices Require Licenses.
  2. Select the device(s) you want to apply a license key.
  3. Click Apply Licenses. The Apply Licenses to Device page displays.
  4. Click the Apply License link below each section to apply license keys to the selected devices.
  5. Click Finish.

Unassigning License Keys from Devices

  1. On the Account Home page, under Device Management, click Assigned & Licensed. The list of all the devices with assignments and licenses in your account displays.
  2. Select the device(s) you want to unassign from devices.
  3. Click Detach License.

    4. A confirming message displays asking if you are sure you wish to proceed with detaching the license key from these devices. Detaching license keys from devices can disrupt service and cause network outages.

  4. Click Detach License again to continue.

Adding License Keys to your Account

You add a device license key to your account independent of adding a specific device.

Use the following steps to add extra license keys to your account.

  1. On the Account Home page, under Device Management, click Add License Keys.
  2. In the Add Licenses dialog, enter the license key.
  3. Click Enter. Continue adding license keys.
  4. Click Add Licenses when you are finished adding keys.

The license(s) are now available for you to assign to devices in your account.

Downloading Device List

  1. On the Account Home page, click Device Management. The Device Management (Inventory) page displays. The device list is a complete view of all your devices and services.
  2. Click Download Device List. The Download Device List dialog box displays.
  3. Select the file type you want to export. HPE CCS supports two file types:
    • CSV
    • PDF
  4. Click Download. Details for the devices you selected downloads.
